The Light Shines over the Darkness

Captured with Canon EOS Rebel T3I

Punchline - A hero is born as the light shines over the darkness.

The South Bronx is an area famous for gang violence and the drug trade. The light shines as if to say there is still hope. Maybe a hero was born (or conceived) as I took the photo. But we'll never know.
