My Nighthawks

Modification of a classic painting

Punchline - Who's that handsome fellow at the bar?

Through the manipulation of elements already in the image as well as an image of myself, I was able to input myself into a classic painting. Nighthawks, the classic painting by Edward Hopper, is one of the most recognizable pieces of art. So when you create such a subtle change like having an extra person sitting at the bar, it creates inquiry. Wet Willies in Miami has a cup with their version of Nighthawks. That 70's show has a scene where they zoom out and the scene resembles Nighthawks. I wanted to make my own version of Nighthawks and this was the product.
