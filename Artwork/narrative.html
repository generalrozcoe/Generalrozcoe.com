Continuous Narrative

Captured with Canon EOS Rebel T3I

Punchline - What is so important that I had to watch it 3 times?

Through the use of masking and a tripod, I was able to take multiple photos of myself in various positions and then composite them to make one photo. The final product looks as though something incredibly important is occuring on my computer screen. I am attempting to recreate a form of art used thousands of years ago where an artist would have a character represented twice in the image to show the progression of the story.
